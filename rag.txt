Yehi Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) ka complete workflow hai. Dhyan dene wali baat ye hai ki LLM (Gemini) search nahi karta—search ka kaam retriever aur vector database karte hain. Gemini ko bas relevant context diya jata hai, aur us context ke basis par final answer generate karne ka kaam Gemini karta hai. Yahi wajah hai ki RAG systems large documents aur enterprise applications me itne effective hote hain.
